## LinkedIn Post Draft

### Topic: Automating Client Communication with AI

### Content

Most businesses lose hours every week to emails that follow the same pattern every single time.

I recently built an AI Employee that handles my inbox end-to-end — reading emails, assessing priority, drafting replies, and flagging anything that needs my decision. An urgent invoice request from a client? The AI drafts the acknowledgment, creates an approval request with the amount and context, and waits for me to say go. The email is out in minutes, not hours.

The real insight is this: the goal isn't to remove humans from the loop — it's to remove humans from the *repetitive* parts of the loop. I still approve every financial commitment and every reply that goes out. But I'm no longer the bottleneck on the routine stuff.

The practical takeaway: map out the 5 emails you send most often. If they follow a template, an AI can draft them. You review, you approve, you send. That's it. Your time goes to the 10% that actually needs your thinking.

What's one task in your business you could hand off to an AI this week?
